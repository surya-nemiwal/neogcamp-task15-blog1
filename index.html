Okay, so lets get started then

1. No-game-No-Life

The first in our list is a short anime called "No Game No Life". If you are one of the folks who likes strategy and mindgame series then you would love this anime.

The plot of this anime revolves around a brother and sister duo who are diehard gamers and on one day are transported to a world where people use games instead of strength to solve their mutual disputes. So the anime is all about the fun and mindblowing game strategies that the duo uses to help humanity in the transported world.

2. Hunter X Hunter

The story of this anime focuses on a 12 year old boy named Gon Freecss who was once abandoned by his father when he was very small. Even after knowing that the path to find his father is very tough, Gon sets out on a mission to meet him someday.

So the story of this anime revloves around Gon and all his friends that he made on various adventurious jurnies to accomplish his only dream of meeting his father someday.

If you love going on action adventures then this is a must watch for you.

3. Demon Slayer

This is a story about a boy name Tanjirou who after his father's death works hard to support his family by selling charcoal. But on one morning his entire life flips upside down when he finds out that his entire family is killed my a demon except his younger sister Nezuko.

Though Nezuko survives the demon attack but she gets turned into a demon. On knowing this Tanjirou decides no matter what he will turn her sister back to normaland the plot follows.

If you want some really good animated and directed anime that and too with a awesome story plot than this anime is for you
